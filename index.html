Bio:

Hailing from Basel, Switzerland, the black metal band Hån (Norwegian, "scorn") was founded in 2009 and has eversince been active, releasing a self-produced demo in 2012 and bringing mayhem to numerous stages with their raw, in-your-face blasphemy, supporting major black metal acts across Europe.

The band released their first full length - Facilis Descensus Averni - via Northern Silence Productions!

Current members are Gnist (vocals), Lodur (guitar), Kryptos (guitar), Grevling (drums), Jehoruan (bass)

On 2021-01-29 the next full length - Breathing the Void - will be released via Norther Silence Productions:
